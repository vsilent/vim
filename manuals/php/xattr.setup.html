Требования

To use xattr, you will need libattr installed. It is available at » http://oss.sgi.com/projects/xfs/.

Замечание: These functions only work on filesystems that support extended attributes, and have them enabled at mount time. Some common filesystems that support extended attributes are ext2, ext3, reiserfs, jfs, and xfs.
